About Us

Our Business

CornerStone Agile was started in response to the high demand of organizations wishing to migrate away from traditional "waterfall" methods and embrace the success that can come from implementing Agile and Scrum in their software development process.

All too often, we hear about companies saying that they're "going Agile", but in reality, when you look under the hood, they've created a hybrid approach that's simply another way of implementing "timebox waterfall".

Having been employed by several companies that have gone through the painful process (and it IS painful) of implementing Agile methods, and the Scrum framework in particular, we realized that we can help minimize that pain for other companies by shepparding them through the process.

Why reinvent the wheel? Work with professionals who do this everyday!
