Services

Executive Briefings

Help leadership understand the benefits of investing in, and supporting, Agile methods


Organizational Discovery and Assessment

Provide independent evaluation and observations of issues, risks, impediments and recommendations for transforming into an Agile organization while helping to identify those team members that might be best suitable to fill the roles of Scrum Master, Product Owner and Scrum Team for your initial pilot project.


Agile Workshops

From Introductory Agile classroom instruction to advanced, personalized, topic specific training to instruct teams in the understanding, practice and implementation of the Scrum framework for managing software development projects.

Agile Jumpstart

Provide on-site Scrum Master and/or Product Owner services to get Agile practices started immediately on your critical projects. Fosters a “learn by example” apprenticeship approach to helping team members become familiar with Agile methods while recommending options for creating physical environments conducive to collaboration.

Agile Technology Consulting

From Atlassian JIRA AGILE installation and workflow configuration to end-to-end tool chain integration with Stash, Git/GitHub, Bamboo, and Maven, we help teams engage in Acceptance Test / Behavior Driven Development, Automated Testing, Continuous Integration and Automated Deployment through the entire Agile project lifecycle.

Integrated Agile Team Coaching

Provide on-site leadership, guidance and support to evaluate and help existing teams bring their Agile practice to the next level through practice validation.
